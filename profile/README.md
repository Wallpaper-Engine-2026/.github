# Wallpaper Engine by [Wallpaper Engine Team]

Use stunning live wallpapers on your desktop. Animate your own images, import videos or websites, and share them on the Steam Workshop!

---

## What is Wallpaper Engine?

**Wallpaper Engine** lets you use live wallpapers on your Windows desktop. It supports many types of animated wallpapers:

- 3D and 2D animations  
- Websites  
- Videos  
- Certain applications  

You can pick an existing wallpaper or create your own. Share your creations on the Steam Workshop!

**New:** Use the free Android companion app to transfer wallpapers to your Android device.

## Features

- Bring your desktop to life with real-time graphics, videos, applications, or websites.
- Use animated screensavers when you're away.
- Customize wallpapers with your favorite colors.
- Use interactive wallpapers controlled by your mouse.
- Supports many resolutions and aspect ratios: 16:9, 21:9, 16:10, 4:3.
- Works with multi-monitor setups.
- Wallpapers pause while gaming to save performance.
- Create your own wallpapers in the built-in editor.
- Animate wallpapers from images, HTML, or video files.
- Share and download wallpapers on the Steam Workshop.
- Works alongside other Steam games or apps.
- Supported video formats: `.mp4`, `.webm`, `.avi`, `.m4v`, `.mov`, `.wmv` (only `.mp4` for Workshop).
- Companion Android app lets you take wallpapers on the go.
- Supports Razer Chroma and Corsair iCUE.

---

## Performance

Wallpaper Engine is designed to use minimal system resources.

- Automatically pause or stop wallpapers when running fullscreen apps or games.
- Many settings to fine-tune performance and quality.
- Best performance with 3D, 2D, and video wallpapers.
- Websites and apps may use more system resources.
- A dedicated GPU is recommended but not required.
